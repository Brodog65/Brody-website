Fortnite Skins TEST

"Outfits (Informally referred to as Skins) are a type of Cosmetic Item players may equip and use for Fortnite: Battle Royale, Fortnite: Creative, and Fortnite: Save the World. They enable the player to change the model and aesthetic of the player character, with no competitive advantage, except for blocking ADS. "
